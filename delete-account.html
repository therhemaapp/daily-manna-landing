Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly in the app or by contacting us.

Option 1 — Delete in the App

1 Open the Daily Manna app and go to the Profile tab.
2 Scroll down to the Danger Zone section.
3 Tap "Delete Account" and confirm the action.

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Contact Us

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by sending an email to:

therhema.app@gmail.com

Please include the email address or Google/Apple account associated with your Daily Manna account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

What Data Is Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your display name and account preferences
  • Your Firebase authentication record
  • Your FCM push notification token
  • Any referral or premium status linked to your account

Anonymous analytics data (non-identifiable) may be retained as required by Google Firebase's data retention policies.
